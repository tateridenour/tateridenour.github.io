SYNERGY WEBSITE
web design

Synergy is a graphic design exhibition for a class of newly graduating students. Various brand elements had already been chosen, which specified the color scheme, the logo, and the use of gradients. Given these elements, I was tasked with integrating them into an interactive website to showcase the portfolios of the students and give information about the event.

I knew that an exhibition in a creative field would allow for creative risks, so the website includes things such as a flashy opening animation, a visual structure based on gradients, and an unconventional layout. The student pages have a simpler design as to not take attention away from the student work.

Designs were wireframed in Figma, then programmed with raw HTML and CSS. Great care was put into responsive design for mobile.

LEXICON
app design

An app for teaching people how to play “cryptic crosswords”, a type of crossword focused on misleading wordplay that has a high barrier to entry. The app uses the metaphor of a flower blooming to represent skill growth.
